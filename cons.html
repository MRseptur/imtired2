The Cons of NOT sleeping

the Consiquenses of not sleep

Reduced cognitive function: Sleep deprivation can impair your ability to think, concentrate, and make decisions, affecting your overall cognitive function. Weakened immune system: Sleep deprivation can weaken your immune system, making you more susceptible to illness and infections. Impaired memory: Sleep plays a critical role in consolidating memories, so not getting enough sleep can make it difficult to remember things. Mood swings: Sleep deprivation can cause irritability, mood swings, and even depression. Impaired judgment: Sleep deprivation can impair judgment, making it difficult to make rational decisions.
